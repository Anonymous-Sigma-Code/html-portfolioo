Joshua's Top 3 Movies of ALL TIME

These are movies you should definitely watch

1. The Pirates of the Carribean: Curse of the Black Pearl

This movie is somewhat considered a classic, starring classic actors such as Johnny Depp and Geoffrey Rush. I really like the silly, simple humour of this film.

2. Big Hero 6

A very cool animated movie. I really love the idea behind the movie that some science geeks can become superheroes.

3. Fast & the Furious: Tokyo Drift

If you love fast cars doing incredible stunts, then this is the movie for you. It is also set in the beautiful scenery of tokyo, Japan.
